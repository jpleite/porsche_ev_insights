# Porsche EV Insights

Welcome to the Porsche EV Insights wiki. This documentation will help you get the most out of the dashboard for analyzing your Porsche EV or Audi e-tron GT trip data.

## What is Porsche EV Insights?

Porsche EV Insights is a privacy-first web application that transforms the trip data exported from the My Porsche app into meaningful analytics about your driving habits, efficiency, costs, and environmental impact. All data processing happens entirely in your browser - your data never leaves your device.

## Supported Vehicles

The dashboard supports all Porsche electric vehicles and the Audi e-tron GT:

**Porsche**
- **Taycan Sedan** - Base RWD, 4, 4S, GTS, Turbo, Turbo S, Turbo GT
- **Taycan Cross Turismo** - 4, 4S, GTS, Turbo, Turbo S
- **Taycan Sport Turismo** - 4S, GTS
- **Macan Electric** - Base, 4, 4S, Turbo
- **Cayenne Electric** - Base, Turbo

**Audi** (J1 platform - same as Taycan)
- **e-tron GT** - Base, S, RS, RS performance (2021-2024 and 2025+ generations)

## Quick Start

1. Export your trip data from the My Porsche app (see [Exporting Data from My Porsche](Exporting-Data-from-My-Porsche))
2. Visit the dashboard at [https://jpleite.github.io/porsche_ev_insights](https://jpleite.github.io/porsche_ev_insights)
3. Upload your CSV file(s)
4. Explore your driving analytics

## Privacy First

Your data security is paramount. Porsche EV Insights:

- Processes all data entirely in your browser
- Never sends your trip data to any server
- Stores data only in your browser's local storage
- Allows you to delete all data at any time
- Works completely offline after initial load
